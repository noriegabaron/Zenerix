HOUSE RULES


1. We may allow an early check-in or late check-out depending on availability and with an additional fee which should be agreed upon with the host beforehand.

2. Guests should bring their own bath towels for personal hygiene purposes.

3. We only accommodate a maximum of 20 persons for this house including children.

4. Smoking is strictly prohibited inside the house.

5. Basic kitchen and dining utilities are available for use by the guest, but it is the guest's responsibility to clean them after use.

6. Room service/ house help/ cook/ dishwasher is not provided as the property is NOT a hotel or resort.

8. Treat the unit as your own home. Keep the property and all facilities/amenities in proper order and good condition. We ask that you respect our property and leave the place as you found it as we really work hard in maintaining everything in our property in very good condition.
